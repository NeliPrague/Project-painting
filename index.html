About me

My name is Walter Peña. I am 30 year's old sport teacher from Venezuela. I moved to Prague one year ago, because my wife is Czech. Paiting is my hobby and the time to relax. I started to paint by cacao and coffee, because I produced them in my farm in my home town Merida in Venezuela and I would like my painting to smell very good.
